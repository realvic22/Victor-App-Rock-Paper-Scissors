How to Play

  • Choose your move – Click on Rock 🪨, Paper 📄, or Scissors ✂️.
  • The computer makes its choice – It will randomly pick Rock, Paper, or Scissors.
  • See the result – The game follows these simple rules:
  • Rock crushes Scissors 🪨✂️ → You win!
  • Scissors cut Paper ✂️📄 → You win!
  • Paper covers Rock 📄🪨 → You win!
  • If you and the computer pick the same, it's a tie! 🤝
